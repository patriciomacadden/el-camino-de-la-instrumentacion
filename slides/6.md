# Available Templates

## Frontpage Template
Perfect for welcome slides and titles
```markdown
---
template: frontpage
---

# Your Presentation Title
## Subtitle Here
```

## Section Title Template
Great for chapter dividers
```markdown
---
template: section-title
---

# Chapter Name
## Chapter Description
```

## Content Template
Main slides with text, code, images
```markdown
---
template: content
---

# Slide Title
Your content goes here!
```
